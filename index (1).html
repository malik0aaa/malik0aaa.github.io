about me

Hey there! I'm Malika, a Computer Science student specializing in Data Analytics at Sheridan College, originally from Saudi Arabia and now building my career in Canada.

My path into tech wasn't straight: I tried engineering, shifted to IT, and somewhere along the way became genuinely fascinated with how systems work from the ground up. Programming doesn't come easy by default, but that's never been the point. It's the challenge of hitting a wall and figuring your way through it that keeps me going.

I'm driven by tangible impact and I don't shy away from structure, I actually thrive in it. Whether it's a team project or an independent build, I show up to get things done. Currently developing my skills in Python, SQL, and data visualization, and working toward a Power BI certification. Fuelled, as always, by an unreasonable amount of coffee. ☕
